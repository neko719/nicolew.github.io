Find The Best Way to Learn Languages

CanTalk is a user-friendly language learning app with intuitive design and smooth interactions that enhance your communication experience.

Try CanTalk

Assignment Context

GBDA 210 - Introduction to User Experience Design. A comprehensive project focused on solving real-world communication barriers through mobile app design.

My Role

UX Designer & Prototyper. Managed the end-to-end design process, from initial user research and wireframing to creating an interactive high-fidelity prototype.

Problem & Objective

Many language learners struggle with the gap between vocabulary apps and real-life conversation. CanTalk's objective is to bridge this gap through scenario-based learning and intuitive community interactions.

Impact & Reflection

The final prototype received high praise for its smooth navigation. I gained a deep understanding of the iterative design process—testing low-fi sketches before committing to high-fi visuals.
