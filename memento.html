UX / UI DESIGN CASE STUDY

Capture Moments
with Meaning

Memento is a digital memory archive designed to help users document personal experiences through visual storytelling and mindful reflection.

Try Memento Prototype

Assignment Context

BET 350 - Technology Entrepreneurship. Focused on identifying market gaps and building a tech-based solution through entrepreneurial thinking.

My Role

UX/UI Designer & Researcher. Responsible for user persona development, journey mapping, and creating high-fidelity interactive prototypes.

Problem & Objective

In a fast-paced world, people "collect" photos but rarely "reflect" on them. Memento turns digital clutter into a mindful archive for growth.

Impact & Reflection

Validated product-market fit through interviews. I learned to balance aesthetic minimalism with complex functional needs of a digital archive.

User Insights & Journey

Customer Profile

Outlining target users' motivations and behaviors to inform empathetic design decisions.

Customer Journey

Mapping emotional touchpoints and usability priorities from discovery to long-term engagement.
