Capture Moments with Meaning

Memento is a digital memory archive designed to help users document personal experiences through visual storytelling and mindful reflection.

Try Memento

Assignment Context

BET 350 - Technology Entrepreneurship. Focused on identifying market gaps and building a tech-based solution.

My Role

UX/UI Designer & Researcher. Responsible for user persona development, journey mapping, and high-fidelity prototyping in Figma.

Problem & Objective

In a fast-paced digital world, people often "collect" photos but rarely "reflect" on them. Memento aims to turn passive digital clutter into a mindful archive for personal growth.

Impact & Reflection

Successfully validated the product-market fit through user interviews. I learned how to balance aesthetic minimalism with the complex functional needs of a digital archive.

Customer Profile

The customer profile outlines Memento’s target users, including their motivations, behaviors, and needs. It helps inform design decisions to better meet user expectations.

Customer Journey

This journey maps out the key stages users experience when engaging with Memento — from discovering the app to reflecting on their personal growth. It highlights emotional touchpoints, usability priorities, and engagement opportunities.
